Project Overview

This project explores hybrid learning in artificial ecosystems, combining deep reinforcement learning with evolutionary algorithms. Agents process their environment with spatial (CNN) and internal state (MLP) inputs, adapting both within episodes and across generations.

Results and Impact

The hybrid system outperforms standalone EA and RL models in fitness, adaptability, and behavioral complexity. The project offers a foundation for future research in artificial life, game AI, and education.
